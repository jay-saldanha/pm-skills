# Filler Phrases to Cut

## Throat-clearing openers
- "It's worth noting that..."
- "It's important to understand that..."
- "Let's dive into..."
- "Let's explore..."
- "In today's world..."
- "In today's fast-paced environment..."
- "Now more than ever..."
- "At the end of the day..."
- "The fact of the matter is..."
- "Here's the thing:"
- "The truth is..."
- "Make no mistake:"
- "To be clear:"
- "Simply put:"
- "In other words:"
- "That said,"
- "With that in mind,"
- "Having said that,"
- "It goes without saying that..."
- "Needless to say,"

## Emphasis crutches (almost always cut)
- truly
- really
- deeply
- incredibly
- fundamentally
- essentially
- basically
- literally
- absolutely
- certainly
- definitely
- obviously
- clearly
- simply
- just (when used for emphasis)
- quite
- rather
- somewhat
- very

## Transition filler
- "This is why..."
- "And that's exactly..."
- "Which brings us to..."
- "So what does this mean?"
- "Here's what that looks like:"
- "Here's the key:"
- "The bottom line:"
- "At its core,"
- "In essence,"
- "When all is said and done,"

## Closing clichés
- "...and that makes all the difference."
- "...and that changes everything."
- "That's the power of X."
- "This is how X works."
- "The rest is history."
